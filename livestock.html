Livestock in Ireland

In GEOG 370: Introduction to Cartography, for the final project in this course, we were tasked with demonstrating our understanding of cartographic design principles and best practices by executing a complete map design, from concept to final delivery. The project emphasized developing proficiency in the ArcGIS Pro to Adobe Illustrator workflow, bridging technical GIS skills with graphic design tools. For my map, I created a bivariate visualization of livestock distribution across counties in the Republic of Ireland, highlighting spatial patterns using thoughtful symbolization and layout.
