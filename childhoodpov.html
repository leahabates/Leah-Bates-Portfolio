Child Poverty Rates in Wisconsin

In GEOG 575: Interactive Cartography & Geovisualization, our first project involved creating a space-time proportional symbol map. I focused on childhood poverty rates across Wisconsin, using data from the U.S. Census Bureau. The map was developed with the Leaflet library and allows users to interact with the data over time using a slider and control buttons to explore changes across different years.
