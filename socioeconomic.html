Socioeconomic Health Indicators in California

In GEOG 575: Interactive Cartography & Geovisualization, our second project focused on creating a coordinated visualization map. For my map, I explored the relationship between wealth and health inequalities by leveraging health and social indicators. Using California as a case study, I illustrated how significant disparities in health outcomes persist even within a single, economically diverse state. The visualization was built using the D3.js library and data sourced from the U.S. Census Bureau. Users can interact with the map by selecting different variables from a dropdown menu, allowing them to explore how various attributes shift across the state.
