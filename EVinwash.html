Where are the Electrc Cars?

In GEOG 370: Introduction to Cartography, this was the third project aimed to challenged us to create a compelling thematic map using a classed choropleth technique while applying best practices in cartographic design. The objective was to communicate a clear visual argument through thoughtful data normalization, s ymbolization, and layout. For my map, I explored electric vehicle (EV) adoption across counties in Washington State, highlighting regional trends and disparities in sustainable transportation. The final d esign was developed using an ArcGIS Pro to Adobe Illustrator workflow, incorporating techniques in generalization, typography, and projection. The project was styled as a submission to the Atlas of Design, emphasizing both visual clarity and narrative impact.
