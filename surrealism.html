Surrealims Map

In GEOG 572: Graphic Design in Cartography, our third project focused on designing a custom styled tileset. The assignment involved deconstructing an existing aesthetic and applying its stylistic components—such as form, color, type, and texture—to a multiscale “slippy” tileset. This was developed using Mapbox, with the goal of creating a cohesive visual style that remained consistent across all zoom levels of the basemap.
